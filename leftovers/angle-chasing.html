Angle Chasing

Question 1

The degree measure of angle $A$ is

a. $20$

b. $30$

c. $35$

d. $40$

e.$45$

Question 2

In the quadrilateral $ABCD$, $\overline{AB}$ is parallel to $\overline{CD}$, $CD = 2AB$, $\angle ADC=30^{\circ}$ and $\angle BCD = 50^{\circ}$. Let $M$ be the midpoint of $\overline{CD}$. The measure of $\angle AMB$ is

a. $80^{\circ}$

b. $90^{\circ}$

c. $100^{\circ}$

d. $110^{\circ}$

e.$120^{\circ}$

Question 3

Four of the angles of a convex polygon are each equal to $100^{\circ}$. The smallest possible degree measure of any of the remaining angles lies in which of the following intervals?

a. $(105,120)$

b. $(120,135)$

c. $(135,150)$

d. $(150,165)$

e.$(165,180)$

Question 4

Rectangle $ABCD$ has $AB = 6$ and $BC = 3$. Point $M$ is chosen on side $AB$ so that $\angle AMD=\angle CMD$. What is the degree measure of $\angle AMD$ ?

a. $15$

b. $30$

c. $45$

d. $60$

e.$75$
