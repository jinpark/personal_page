Question 1

a. $-2$

b. $2$

c. $2-2p$

d. $2p-2$

e.$|2p-2|$

Question 2

a. $ - 1 < x < 2$

b. $0 < x < 1$

c. $ - 1 < x < 3$

d. $x < 2$

e.$x < - 1\,\,{\text{or }}x > 2$
