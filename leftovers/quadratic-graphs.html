Quadratic Graphs

Question 1

The vertex of the parabola $y = x^2 - 8x + c$ will be a point on the $x$-axis if the value of $c$ is

a. $-16$

b. $-4$

c. $4$

d. $8$

e.$16$

Question 2

Consider the graphs of

(1) $y=x^2-\dfrac{1}{2}x+2$

(2) $y=x^2+\dfrac{1}{2}x+2$

on the same set of axes. Which of the following statements is true?

a. The graphs coincide.

b. The graph of (1) is lower than the graph of (2).

c. The graph of (1) is to the left of the graph of (2).

d. The graph of (1) is to the right of the graph of (2).

e.The graph of (1) is higher than the graph of (2).

Question 3

A parabola has vertex at $(4,-5)$ and has two $x$-intercepts, one positive and one negative. If this parabola is the graph of $y=ax^2+bx+c$, which of $a$, $b$, and $c$ must be positive?

a. only $a$

b. only $b$

c. only $c$

d. $a$ and $b$ only

e.none

Question 4

A parabola with equation $y=ax^2+bx+c$ is reflected about the $x$-axis. The parabola and its reflection are translated horizontally five units in opposite directions to become the graphs of $y = f(x)$ and $y = g(x)$, respectively. Which of the following describes the graph of $y = (f+g)(x)$?

a. a parabola tangent to the $x$-axis

b. a parabola not tangent to the $x$-axis

c. a horizontal line

d. a non-horizontal line

e.the graph of a cubic function

Question 5

a. $\dfrac{1}2$

b. $\dfrac{9}{16}$

c. $\dfrac{19}{32}$

d. $\dfrac{5}{8}$

e.$\dfrac23$
